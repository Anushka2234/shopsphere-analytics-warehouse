# Business Requirements

## Objective

Build an analytics warehouse capable of answering business questions.

---

## Business Questions

1. What are the total sales?

2. Which products generate the highest revenue?

3. Which customers purchase the most?

4. Which sellers generate the highest sales?

5. Which states have the highest revenue?

6. Monthly sales trend

7. Daily order volume

8. Average order value

9. Product category performance

10. Payment method analysis

11. Delivery performance

12. Customer purchase behavior

---

## KPIs

- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Average Freight
- Total Customers
- Total Sellers
- Total Products
- Revenue by Category
- Revenue by State
