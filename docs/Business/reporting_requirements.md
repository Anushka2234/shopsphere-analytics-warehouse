# Reporting Requirements Document

Project:
ShopSphere Enterprise Analytics Warehouse


Purpose:

Define dashboards, KPIs, filters, users, and business questions required for analytics reporting.

# Dashboard Scope


The analytics platform will contain five dashboards:


1. Executive Dashboard

2. Sales Performance Dashboard

3. Customer Analytics Dashboard

4. Product Analytics Dashboard

5. Operations Dashboard


# 1. Executive Dashboard


Users:

CEO

Senior Management


Purpose:

Provide high-level company performance overview.


Business Questions:

- How is the company performing?
- Are sales increasing?
- Which regions generate revenue?
- Are customers growing?


KPIs:

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Revenue Growth %
- Average Order Value


Charts:


KPI Cards:

Revenue

Orders

Customers


Line Chart:

Monthly Sales Trend


Map:

Revenue by Location


Bar Chart:

Top Categories


Filters:

Year

Month

Region

 2. Sales Performance Dashboard

- Average Sales

- Quantity Sold

Sales by Category


Table:

Category

Seller

Location
