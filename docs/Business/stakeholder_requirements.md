# Stakeholder Requirements Document

Project:
ShopSphere Enterprise Analytics Warehouse


Purpose:

This document identifies business stakeholders,
their reporting needs, KPIs, and analytics requirements

## 1. Executive Team

Roles:

CEO
COO
Business Leadership


Responsibilities:

Monitor overall company performance and growth.


Business Questions:

- How much revenue are we generating?
- Are sales increasing or decreasing?
- Which regions perform best?
- Are customers growing?


Required Metrics:

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Revenue Growth %
- Active Customers
- Average Order Value


Dashboard Required:

Executive Dashboard


# 2. Sales Team


Responsibilities:

Analyze sales performance.


Business Questions:

- What are monthly sales trends?
- Which products sell the most?
- Which sellers generate maximum revenue?
- Which regions need improvement?


Required Metrics:

- Total Sales
- Number of Orders
- Sales Growth
- Top Products
- Top Sellers


Dashboard Required:

Sales Performance Dashboard


## 3. Marketing Team


Responsibilities:

Understand customers and improve campaigns.


Business Questions:

- Who are our best customers?
- Where are customers located?
- How often do customers purchase?
- What products do customers prefer?


Required Metrics:

- Customer Count
- Repeat Customers
- Customer Segments
- Customer Lifetime Value


Dashboard Required:

Customer Analytics Dashboard


## 4. Product Management Team


Responsibilities:

Monitor product performance.


Business Questions:

- Which products generate revenue?
- Which categories perform best?
- Which products receive poor ratings?


Required Metrics:

- Product Revenue
- Quantity Sold
- Product Ranking
- Average Review Score


Dashboard Required:

Product Analytics Dashboard

## 5. Operations Team


Responsibilities:

Manage delivery and order operations.


Business Questions:

- Are orders delivered on time?
- Which locations have delays?
- How many orders are cancelled?


Required Metrics:

- Delivered Orders
- Cancelled Orders
- Average Delivery Time
- Late Delivery Rate


Dashboard Required:

Operations Dashboard

## 6. Finance Team


Responsibilities:

Track financial performance.


Business Questions:

- What is total revenue?
- What are payment trends?
- What is average transaction value?


Required Metrics:

- Revenue
- Payment Amount
- Average Order Value
- Monthly Revenue


Dashboard Required:

Financial Dashboard
