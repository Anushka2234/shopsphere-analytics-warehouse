# ShopSphere Analytics Warehouse
# Business Case Document


## 1. Company Overview

ShopSphere is a growing e-commerce company that allows customers to purchase products from multiple sellers across different locations.

The company manages thousands of daily transactions including:

- Customer orders
- Product sales
- Payments
- Deliveries
- Customer reviews


## 2. Current Situation

Currently business teams depend on multiple operational systems and manual reports.

Sales, marketing, finance, and operations teams maintain separate reports, causing inconsistent business metrics.


## 3. Business Challenges

### Problem 1:
Manual reporting process

Impact:
Reports take longer to prepare and delay decision making.


### Problem 2:
No centralized analytics system

Impact:
Different departments calculate KPIs differently.


### Problem 3:
Limited customer insights

Impact:
Marketing teams cannot effectively understand customer behavior.


### Problem 4:
Product performance visibility issues

Impact:
Business teams cannot easily identify successful and underperforming products.


## 4. Proposed Solution

Build an enterprise data warehouse solution.

The system will:

- Extract data from operational sources
- Clean and transform data
- Store analytics-ready information
- Provide Power BI dashboards


## 5. Expected Benefits

- Single source of truth
- Faster reporting
- Better customer analysis
- Improved sales visibility
- Data-driven decision making
