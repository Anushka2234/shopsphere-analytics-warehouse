# Business Problems


## Problem 1: No Centralized Reporting System


Current Situation:

Different departments maintain separate reports using operational data exports.


Business Impact:

- Different KPI calculations
- Slow reporting process
- Manual Excel dependency


Analytics Requirement:

Create a centralized data warehouse as the single source of truth.


Solution:

Build:

- Fact tables
- Dimension tables
- Power BI dashboards

## Problem 2: Limited Sales Visibility


Current Situation:

Management cannot easily analyze sales performance.


Business Questions:

- How much revenue are we generating?
- Which products sell the most?
- Which regions generate revenue?
- What are monthly sales trends?


Required Metrics:

- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Revenue Growth %

## Problem 3: Poor Customer Understanding


Current Situation:

Customer behavior is not properly analyzed.


Business Questions:

- Who are our valuable customers?
- Where are customers located?
- How frequently do customers purchase?


Required Metrics:

- Total Customers
- Repeat Customers
- Customer Lifetime Value
- Customer Segments

## Problem 4: Product Performance Issues


Current Situation:

Business cannot identify successful and failing products.


Business Questions:

- Which categories perform best?
- Which products have low sales?
- Which products receive poor reviews?


Required Metrics:

- Product Revenue
- Units Sold
- Product Ranking
- Review Score

## Problem 5: Delivery Performance Issues


Current Situation:

Operations team lacks delivery insights.


Business Questions:

- Are customers receiving orders on time?
- Which areas experience delays?


Required Metrics:

- Average Delivery Time
- Late Deliveries
- Cancelled Orders
